What is PLANARIZE?

A form of interactive platfrom between customer and service providers that help both the parties in getting and providing the best services respectively. With planarize, a customer can book any sort of events by interacting through the services made availaible by the service providers. Planarize gives the flexibilty to book or decline the events to both customers and service providers. We at planarize aim to make both the parties happy!!
